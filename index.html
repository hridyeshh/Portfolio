I'm an avid reader with a particular love for productivity literature, poetry, and the art of writing—interests that profoundly shape how I approach problem-solving and communication in tech.

My fascination with storytelling isn't just a hobby—it's a superpower in the development world. Whether I'm documenting complex systems, presenting technical concepts to stakeholders, or collaborating with cross-functional teams, I bring the narrative clarity that turns confusion into understanding. These diverse interests don't distract from my technical work—they enhance it, bringing creativity, empathy, and clear communication to everything I build.
